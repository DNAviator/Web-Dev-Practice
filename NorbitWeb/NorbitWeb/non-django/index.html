Welcome to Norbit!

Welcome to Norbit Engineering, where big dreams meet youthful ingenuity! We're a team of four passionate high schoolers dedicated to turning your ideas into reality. Led by Ryan (worksite manager), Caleb (chief engineering officer), Molly (head of the art department), and Joseph (CEO), we bring a unique blend of technical expertise and creative vision to every project. Whether you need a prototype for a revolutionary invention, a custom-designed space for your home, or a solution to a seemingly impossible challenge, we're here to listen, collaborate, and bring your vision to life. We believe in the power of collaboration and embrace unconventional approaches, ensuring every project is tackled with fresh perspectives and boundless enthusiasm.
