About Us

Norbit Engineering: Where Youthful Ingenuity Meets Inventive Solutions


We're not your average engineering firm. We're a team of four passionate high schoolers - Ryan (worksite manager), Caleb (chief engineering officer), Molly (head of the art department), and Joseph (CEO) - who believe that age is just a number when it comes to innovation. We're driven by a shared passion for problem-solving and a desire to make a difference, and our youthful energy translates into dynamic brainstorming sessions and a fearless approach to tackling challenges.

From Lockpicking to Lunar Rovers: A Glimpse into Our Past Projects


Our journey began with a seemingly simple request - a custom lock that defied conventional design. Utilizing our diverse skillsets, we combined intricate mechanics with artistic flair, creating a unique and secure solution. This initial project sparked a fire within us, and soon, we were tackling increasingly complex challenges.

Next, we set our sights on harnessing the power of gravity. Our gravity-driven vehicle, fueled by ingenuity and meticulous calculations, defied expectations and proved that even the most basic principles can yield impressive results. This project not only pushed our engineering expertise but also highlighted our ability to think outside the box.

But our ambitions didn't stop there. We craved a project that truly tested our mettle - and the distance measuring car was born. This intricate machine, equipped with advanced mechanical mechanisms, accurately measured distances, showcasing our dedication to precision and innovation.

These are just a few examples of what we've accomplished. Each project has been a learning experience, pushing us to grow, collaborate, and refine our skills. We believe in learning by doing, and our projects are our classrooms, where we hone our talent and turn dreams into reality.
